Wineries and Restaurants

Luján de Cuyo

It is located approximately 20 km from the city of Mendoza, at about 900 meters above sea level. The vineyard and winery areas are spread throughout the department, including Vistalba, Chacras de Coria, Perdriel, and Agrelo.
Winery visiting hours are generally from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

For a half-day experience, we recommend visiting two wineries during the morning. For a full-day experience, it is possible to visit up to three wineries, depending on each winery’s availability.
