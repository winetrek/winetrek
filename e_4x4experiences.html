"History, Nature and Mystery Along One Scenic Route"

Villavicencio Ghost Town 4x4 Experience

Explore the mountains where time stood still

Travel by 4x4 along the scenic Villavicencio road as we venture into the reserve and admire the striking Termas de Villavicencio Hotel and its captivating gardens. We then continue along the legendary 365 curves route in the Mendoza foothills, where the region’s unique flora and fauna surround us at every turn, until reaching the highest point of the journey: Cruz de Paramillos, offering breathtaking views of the “Colossus of America.”

The adventure continues toward the Paramillos Mines, former mining sites that preserve historical remains and showcase the rich heritage of the region. This experience blends landscapes of outstanding natural and cultural value with spectacular Andean panoramas, creating a deep connection with history and nature. An ideal proposal for travelers seeking adventure, heritage, and the beauty of the mountain environment.
