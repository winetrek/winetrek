Tours in Mendoza

City Tour

Discover the City of Mendoza: History, Nature, and Unforgettable Views.
Our excursion begins by traveling along Mendoza’s wide avenues, lined with its traditional trees and the typical irrigation canals known as acequias, which give life to the city. Our first stop is the Foundational Area of Mendoza, the starting point for discovering the historic center of the old city, where everything began.

From there, we continue to the iconic Independence Square, the city’s central plaza, which connects harmoniously with four other squares that form Mendoza’s distinctive urban layout.
Next, we explore the Civic Center, the administrative heart of the province, before heading into one of Argentina’s most important green spaces: General San Martín Park. Created more than a century ago, this impressive urban park spans 307 hectares of cultivated land, features 17 km of walking paths, and includes 82 additional hectares reserved for future expansion, making it a true source of pride for Mendoza.

The highlight of the tour is the ascent to the imposing Cerro de la Gloria, home to the majestic Monument to the Army of the Andes. From this vantage point, you will enjoy a unique panoramic view of the entire city and its surroundings—the perfect opportunity to capture an unforgettable image of Mendoza.
